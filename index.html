BTT 2019 Basic Theory Test

A Different Way to Study

The first step to get your driving licence in Singapore is to sit and pass your Basic Theory Test (BTT). The BTT 2019 Basic theory test app is designed to let your practice similar test questions that will be coming out.

This method of studying using BTT app is a convenient and interactive way for you to understand and memorise the question.

Features:
- 2 Modes, Practice and Test modes
- More than 400 questions
- Auto marking of test and practice question
- Tracking of marks
- Answers that are entered wrongly, an explanation will be given.
- Ads free BTT 2019 is also available
